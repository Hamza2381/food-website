About Us

Welcome to TheFoodCounter

The Food Counter is a leading provider of ghost and dark kitchen solutions, designed to help food entrepreneurs launch and grow their businesses with ease. We offer state-of-the-art kitchen facilities to support the success of our users in the competitive food industry.
Located in the heart of Birmingham’s bustling Tyseley industrial estate, The Food Counter benefits from a prime position near the main Warwick Road. Our strategic location offers easy access to residential, commercial, and industrial districts, and is conveniently situated less than 2.5 miles from the city centre—outside the ULEZ zone. With direct access to key transport routes such as the A34, A45, and A41, businesses operating from The Food Counter enjoy efficient connectivity across Birmingham and the surrounding areas.
